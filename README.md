## what is it
A multiplayer racing game revolved around the guns held by the characters

### the characters
cof: a little thing holding a rocket launcher
fih: a cute and shotgun-holding fish. the shotgun is used to knockback the fish or other players
frog: a frog with a grappling hook wich is the only gun that can't move other players only the one holding it
